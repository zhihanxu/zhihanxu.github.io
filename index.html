Zhihan (Spencer) Xu

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at University of Southern California, starting from Fall 2023. I am fortunate to work with Professor Viktor Prasanna.

My research interests include FPGA accelerator, HLS optimization and HW-SW co-design/optimization.

Previously, I worked with Professor Li Jiang and Professor Zhezhi He on CNN accelerator. I obtained my bachelor's degrees from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China and University of Glasgow, and master's degree from University of Pennsylvania.
